---
name: bug-report-triage
description: Convert a vague bug report into a reproducible, prioritized engineering task.
---

When invoked:

1. Extract:
   - symptoms
   - environment
   - affected area
   - steps to reproduce
   - expected vs actual behavior
2. Identify missing information.
3. Propose likely affected files/modules.
4. Assign a tentative severity:
   - low
   - medium
   - high
   - critical
5. Draft a clean issue summary.

Rules:
- Distinguish evidence from hypothesis.
- Be explicit about unknowns.
