---
name: engineering-report
description: Turn technical work into a clean engineering report structure with consistent notation and concise conclusions.
---

When invoked:

Organize output as relevant:
- Objective
- Method
- Inputs/assumptions
- Results
- Discussion
- Conclusion

Check for:
- notation consistency
- SI units
- variable naming clarity
- unsupported claims

Rules:
- Keep tone technical and clear.
- Prefer direct interpretation of results over filler text.
